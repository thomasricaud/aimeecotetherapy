---
question: "Is therapy reimbursed in France?"
---
Psychotherapy is not reimbursed by the French national health insurance (Sécurité Sociale) unless it is prescribed by a psychiatrist. However, many complementary health plans (mutuelles) offer partial reimbursement for sessions with a registered psychotherapist. It is worth checking your contract or calling your mutuelle to find out. If you are an expatriate, your international health insurance may also cover psychotherapy sessions — check with your provider.
