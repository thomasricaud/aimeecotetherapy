---
question: "What is the difference between a psychologist and a psychopraticien?"
---
The title of psychologue (psychologist) has been protected by law since 1985 and requires a university master's degree (Bac+5). A psychopraticien is a psychotherapy practitioner whose title is freely used and not regulated by the French state, but is governed by professional federations (training, supervision, ethics).
