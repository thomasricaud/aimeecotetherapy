---
question: "What is the difference between a psychologist and a psychotherapist?"
---
A psychologist holds a master's degree in psychology — a protected title regulated by law in France. A psychotherapist is registered on the national register of psychotherapists (RPPS) and can practice psychotherapy after recognised training.
