---
question: "What is the difference between a psychologist and a psychopraticien?"
---
The title of psychologue (psychologist) has been protected by law since 1985 and requires a university master's degree (Bac+5). A psychopraticien is a psychotherapy practitioner whose title is freely used and not regulated by the French state and may or may not have advanced training. Many professionals with degrees in counseling, social work, and marriage and family therapy have to use the title of psychopraticien.
