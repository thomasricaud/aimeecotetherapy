question: "What is the difference between a psychologist and a psychotherapist?"
---
In France, the title of *psychologue* (psychologist) is protected by law and requires a master's degree in psychology from a French university. The title of *psychothérapeute* (psychotherapist) is also regulated and requires registration on the national register (RPPS).

Aimee Cote holds an American degree in systemic therapy and practices as a *psychopraticienne* — a practitioner trained in psychotherapy who works outside the French state-regulated titles. This is common among internationally trained therapists. She specialises in systemic couple and family therapy and conducts sessions in French, English, and Spanish.
